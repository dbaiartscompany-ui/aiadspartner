FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you might want to know about AI search visibility and how AI Ads Partner works.

What is AI search visibility?
AI search visibility is how often a business is named when customers ask AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity for product or service recommendations. Unlike Google search, which displays ten ranked results, AI assistants typically recommend only two to four businesses per answer. A business that is not in those answers is effectively invisible to that customer.
How can I make my business visible in AI search?
AI search visibility is improved through three layers of work. The first is on-site optimization, including structured data, semantic HTML, and content written in a question-and-answer format that AI assistants can quote directly. The second is off-site citation building on sources AI assistants actually read, such as Reddit, Quora, industry publications, and review sites. The third is authority signals through third-party mentions, podcast appearances, and reviews. AI Ads Partner audits your current visibility across all four major AI platforms, then delivers a custom action plan covering all three layers.
What is generative engine optimization (GEO)?
Generative engine optimization, or GEO, is the practice of improving a brand's visibility in answers given by generative AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity. GEO is sometimes called AI SEO or LLM visibility optimization. It differs from traditional SEO because AI assistants synthesize answers from many sources rather than ranking links. GEO focuses on becoming one of the sources AI assistants cite and recommend.

How is this different from traditional SEO?
Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's ranked link results. AI search optimization, or GEO, optimizes for the synthesized answers given by AI assistants. Traditional SEO tactics like backlinks and keywords still matter, but AI assistants weigh different signals: structured data, citation sources, sentiment in reviews, and presence on platforms like Reddit, Quora, and industry publications. AI Ads Partner specializes in this newer layer of discoverability.
